The Artist

When we think of the world of fashion, the first two things that come to mind are models and designers. Behind these people are the make up artists who play an important role as they enhance the model's beauty and complement the designer's creations. Fifi is one of the most sought after in the business by photographers, models and bride's alike. Fifi grew up in Dallas, Texas with Eritrean immigrant parents. Being exposed to different cultures provided her with unique viewpoints and inspirations, which had a direct influence on her body of work. Since 2008, Fifi has worked as a freelance make-up artist for Estee Lauder and MAC Cosmetics and works closely with her team. She frequently styles models for advertisements and represents the company in beauty and fashion events. She is able to make your nose smaller, cheeks thinner, get rid of anything you don't like, and highlight everything you love! Fifi works on her passion daily and has a vision to bring timeless glamour, worldly beauty and enhance the inner beauty of every woman. She uses only the finest make-up and tools in the business.
